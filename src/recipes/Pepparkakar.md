## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb butter 
- 3 1/4 cup flour
- 1 1/2 cup sugar 
- 1 T cinnamon
- 1 T corn syrup 
- 1 T ginger
- 1 T cloves
- 2 tsp baking soda 
- 1 egg 

## Preparation
Juice and grated rind from one orange
1/2 teaspoon salt
Preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit.
Sift flour with soda and spices.
Cream the butter and sugar.
Add syrup and egg.
Add dry ingredients (slowly)
Keep mixing until no lumps/consistent mixture.
Roll the cookies on a lightly floured board.
Dough should be about 1/8' thick.
Cut into shapes.
Bake at 375 for 7 minutes -- cookies should be lightly browned.
